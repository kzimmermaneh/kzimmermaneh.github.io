About Energy Hub

Energy Hub is a marketplace on energy-hub.net that gives oil and gas operators and vendors who serve the Exploration, Production (E&P) and Transport segments easy access to one another. Businesses can now shop and sell on Energy Hub and use purchase orders to improve record keeping.
